Dr. Oleksandr Kazymyrov

Doctor of Philosophy, Candidate of Engineering Sciences

I graduated from Kharkov National University of Radio Electronics (Ukraine), Department of Information Technology Security, in 2010. Started as a research fellow at the Department of Informatics, University of Bergen (Norway), in 2011. As a PhD student at the UoB I do my studies on the topic of symmetric primitives.

My main interests are nonlinear components (substitutions) for modern symmetric algorithms including block ciphers, stream ciphers and hash functions. Moreover, I am also interested in all aspects of information security such as modern trends in designing of the next generation symmetric ciphers, cloud computations and security, secure protocols, computer network security and security analysis of complex systems.
